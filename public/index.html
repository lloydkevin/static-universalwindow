Shutters are our life

Welcome to the premier site dedicated to custom wood plantation shutters. Sure, we sell blinds here and there, but our expertise is in shutters. We love ’em and we’re good at them.

We handle all sizes and types of jobs, but our specialty is designing and building plantation shutters and wood windowtreatments for those “tricky” windows. So, if you need an odd-ball shaped shutter, or have a problem window, give us a call, I’m sure we can design a solution for you-Nothing is impossible, and yes, everything is guaranteed!

What makes us unique

With the help of Computer-Aided Design software and by using our CNC router, we are able to make any kind of shaped shutter imaginable. Nothing is “stock” so everything is custom made for your windows.These are fully functional wood plantation shutters!

Check out our plantation shutter Gallery- We think you’ll agree that we’ve tackled every possible type of shutter situation out there.
